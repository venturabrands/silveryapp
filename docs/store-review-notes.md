# Store Review Notes

## App Summary

Silvery Sleep Expert is an AI-powered sleep coaching app that helps users improve their sleep through personalised tips and habit tracking. It is **not a medical device** and does not diagnose or treat any condition.

## Key Features to Review

1. **Login/Registration** — Email + password authentication (no social login required)
2. **Sleep Chat** — AI-powered conversational sleep guide with streaming responses
3. **Account Management** — View profile, manage chat history, delete conversations
5. **Support & Legal** — Privacy policy, terms of service, medical disclaimer, contact info

## Important Disclaimers

- The app is **not a medical device** and clearly states this in-app (Support page, Disclaimer page, and AI system prompt)
- All users have **free lifetime access** — there are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or payment flows
- Chat content is processed by AI language models but is **not used to train AI models**
- Users can delete individual conversations from their Account page
- Full account deletion is available by emailing support@silvery.com

## Data Handling

- **Collected:** Email, display name, chat messages, sleep diary entries, basic usage analytics
- **Stored:** Encrypted at rest, row-level security ensures users only access their own data
- **Shared:** Chat messages sent to AI providers for processing only (not for training or advertising)
- **Deletion:** Users can delete conversations in-app; full account deletion available via email

## Special Flows

- No in-app purchases or payment flows
- No social login (email/password only)
- No camera, microphone, or location access
- No push notifications
- App works offline for cached pages; chat requires network connectivity (graceful error shown when offline)

## Content Rating

- No violence, gambling, or adult content
- Health & Fitness category
- Age rating: 4+ (iOS) / Everyone (Android)
- No user-generated content visible to other users (all chat is private)
